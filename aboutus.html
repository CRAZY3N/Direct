Why Direct Consulting?

The number of migrants to Mexico grows every year, many of them stay to live in the country, in search of job and business opportunities. For them, the company of Chinese origin, Direct Consulting, created a card that gives them the possibility of accessing a financial product.

It is a plastic with which migrants can make deposits, withdrawals and payments, in addition to creating a credit history to access the services of commercial banking.
